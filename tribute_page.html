Emil Dreptate

A Romanian Poet

Emil Dreptate's published volumes

There is a place

Passing a vapor-like
That will clear the looks tomorrow
You find out the real dimesnsion
of the distances

Restless wake
It is the point on wich
You lean on that moment
Of dream and good Luck

Then a leaf-like
That seasons lose
The memory
Like the mirror

Gets hold of you
The one who knows
That there is a place
Where the eyes

Kept the colors of the world in them
For having looked so much
-A realm where the dreamed of and forgotten dreams
Took Refuge

~Emil Dreptate
